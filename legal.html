Terms and Conditions

  1. Organ donation is a voluntary act and requires the informed consent of the donor.
  2. Donors must be of legal age and capacity to provide consent for organ donation.
  3. Medical evaluations may be required to assess the suitability of the organs for transplantation.
  4. All personal information provided by the donor will be handled in accordance with applicable data protection laws and regulations.

Laws and Actions

  1. The allocation of organs is subject to governmental and ethical guidelines and may vary by region.
  2. In certain cases, family consent may be required, even if the donor has provided consent.
  3. Respect for cultural, religious, and personal beliefs regarding organ donation is essential.
  4. The organization does not endorse or engage in illegal organ trafficking and follows all relevant laws and regulations.
  5. Posthumous organ donation may be an option, and donors can specify their preferences accordingly.
